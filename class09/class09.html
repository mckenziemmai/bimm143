Q24. What original variables are picked up strongly by PC1 in the positive direction? Do these make sense to you?

fruity, pluribus, and hard are all picked up in the positive direction and these do make sense based on the correlation structure in the dataset. If you are a fruity candy you will tend to be hard and come in a packet with multiple candys in it (pluribus).
